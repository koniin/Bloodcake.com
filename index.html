2016-02-23

Evaluating frameworks

I have started evaulating some frameworks for my development. I’ll check into Phaser.io, LibGDX and maybe Haxe/Flixel.
Phaser was really nice to work with in Visual Studio and typescript. Fast development and there is a lot of examples.
I am a little worried about the performance but I don’t know. NW.js works well as it seems.
Haven’t tried publishing to android or ios but I’ve read some about it and it doesn’t seem that bad.
Only thing you read about is people having performance problems which is a little disheartening.
